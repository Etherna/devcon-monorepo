  # Ethereum is the next step of the internet


  It is a decentralized and censorship-resistant platform for social media &
  digital identity, a global financial system with digital banks, and much more.


  Ethereum has a huge ecosystem of infrastructure and decentralized apps and
  there are many developer resources and libraries available.


  Ethereum has a big grants network and many resources to support teams.


  #### [Learn more about Ethereum here](https://ethereum.org/en/)
